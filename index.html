About Me

My name is Chad Chaney and I am currently a student at Northwestern University aiming to finish the Web Development Bootcamp Program in 2017. Formerly, I was a paralegal at major law firms here in Chicago, but after performing mind-numbing tasks that left me feeling unfufilled, I got burned out and started bartending, which turns out to be much more interesting than law.

My aim is to continue in learning computer science and hopefully land a job as a developer. I am well-versed in Python, HTML/CSS and Javascript. In my spare time, I enjoy being with friends, learning more about coding, researching ambient music and consuming art.
